01
Foundation
Brand Clarity
Before you create anything, get clear on who you are right now. Not who you were. Not who you want to be. Right now.
Prompt 01
Who are you and what do you do, in one sentence, no jargon?
Prompt 02
Who specifically are you talking to? Describe one real person.
Prompt 03
What is the one problem you solve better than anyone else?
Prompt 04
What do you want people to say about you after seeing your content?

04
Reflection
Weekly Check-in
Five questions. Answer them every week. Keeps you honest without adding pressure.
📌
1. Did I show up this week? If not, what actually stopped me?
🔥
2. What piece of content performed best, and why do I think it did?
🎯
3. Am I talking to the right person, or am I performing for an audience?
⚙️
4. What do I need to change, drop, or double down on next week?
💡
5. What's one insight from this week I could turn into a post?
