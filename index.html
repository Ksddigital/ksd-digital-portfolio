The Origin Story

I don't just design brands.
I engineer them.

Most marketers start with aesthetics. I started with heavy machinery. With a background in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, my early career wasn't spent picking hex codes, it was spent on the factory floor supervising the remanufacturing of a 49-bus production line.

I learned early that if a system has a bottleneck, production halts. Today, I apply that exact same industrial engineering logic to digital marketing. A beautiful website is useless if the conversion funnel is broken. I build brand identities, SEO strategies, and digital systems that don't just look premium, they operate like well-oiled machines to generate measurable revenue.
